Hi, I'm Stefania — a colour analyst and personal stylist with a lifelong love of style, colour, and helping women feel confident in what they wear.

il colore che ti appartiene

Style isn't what you wear.
It's how you
feel in it.

I grew up in Italy, between Milan and Lake Como, surrounded by beauty, style, and an appreciation for colour and detail. From an early age, I was drawn to how colour can transform not just how something looks, but how it makes you feel.

Before moving to Cambridge, I worked as an event and wedding planner, a role I truly loved. I spent my days helping people bring their vision to life, often through colour — creating palettes, atmospheres, and thoughtful details that felt personal and meaningful. What I enjoyed most was seeing people feel confident, comfortable, and completely themselves in those moments.

Now, as a mum of three, I understand just how much life can shift and evolve. With busy days, changing priorities, and new chapters, it's easy to lose sight of what suits you or what makes you feel like yourself. What once felt effortless can suddenly feel confusing or overwhelming.

That's what led me to colour analysis and personal styling. I wanted to help people reconnect with themselves through what they wear — to rediscover confidence, ease, and a sense of clarity in their style. I create a space where you can explore what truly works for you, so you can feel comfortable, confident, and completely yourself again.

"You already have your own natural beauty. My job is simply to find the colours that let it shine — and watch your confidence follow."
— Stefania

What I bring to
every session.

An Italian eye

Growing up in Italy means growing up with a deep, almost instinctive relationship to colour, proportion, and craft. I don't just analyse — I see. That sensitivity shapes every consultation I do.

The art of the occasion

Years of event planning taught me that how something looks in real life — under real light, with real people — is everything. I bring that same precision to how I dress you for yours.

Quiet confidence

I won't tell you what's trendy. I'll tell you what's yours. My approach is considered, unhurried, and built around you — not a formula, not a seasonal trend cycle.

My approach to colour analysis
& personal styling.

Every session begins with a conversation — because before I look at colours or clothes, I want to understand you. How you live, what you wear on a Tuesday morning, what makes you feel your best. That's the foundation everything else is built on.

For colour analysis, I use a full seasonal draping methodology to identify not just your season, but the specific tones that make your skin luminous, your eyes bright, and your whole appearance come alive. You'll leave with a personalised fabric swatch palette to use every time you shop or get dressed.

For personal styling and wardrobe edits, I take the same unhurried, personal approach — looking at what you have, what works, and what's missing, to help you build a wardrobe that feels intentional, effortless, and completely yours.
