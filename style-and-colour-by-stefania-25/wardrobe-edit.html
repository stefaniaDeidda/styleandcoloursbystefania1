What it's about

A fresh pair of eyes
on everything
you own.

It's easy for a wardrobe to accumulate pieces that no longer fit, no longer suit you, or simply never quite worked in the first place. Over time, that clutter can make getting dressed feel harder than it should — and mean that the pieces you actually love get lost in the noise.

A wardrobe edit is the chance to start fresh. I'll come to you, and together we'll go through everything you have with honest, practical eyes — keeping what works, letting go of what doesn't, and making sure the wardrobe you're left with is one you can actually use.

This isn't about throwing everything away. It's about understanding what you have, how it fits, and how it all works together. By the end of the session, you'll have a clearer picture of your style, a wardrobe that makes sense, and — more often than not — a newfound appreciation for pieces you'd forgotten you owned.

A wardrobe edit also pairs beautifully with a colour analysis or personal styling session. Once you know your colours and style direction, it becomes much easier to see which pieces truly belong in your wardrobe and which ones were never really working for you.
